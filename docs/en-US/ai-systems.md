# AI 系统

## 中文

### 职责

AI 系统负责行为策略、群体运动、响应节奏和环境自适应。

### 规则

- 行为必须可观察。
- 适应必须可追踪。
- 不允许隐性随机掩盖产品问题。
- 行为层必须能够独立测试。

## English

### Responsibility

The AI system owns behavior policy, group motion, response rhythm, and environmental adaptation.

### Rules

- Behavior must be observable.
- Adaptation must be trackable.
- Hidden randomness must not mask product issues.
- The behavior layer must be testable in isolation.
