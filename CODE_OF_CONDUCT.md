# 行为准则 / Code of Conduct

## 中文

### 我们的承诺

NekoDynamics 致力于提供一个专业、克制、尊重事实的协作环境。
我们欢迎围绕性能、架构、交互和可维护性的直接讨论。

### 预期行为

- 尊重他人时间和边界
- 用证据讨论性能和架构问题
- 反馈具体，措辞克制
- 对测试结果和观察保持诚实

### 不可接受行为

- 人身攻击
- 蓄意误导
- 无证据的性能结论
- 破坏性修改

### 执行

维护者可以在必要时移除不符合要求的内容、评论或协作权限。

## English

### Our Commitment

NekoDynamics is committed to a professional, restrained, and evidence-based collaboration environment.
Direct discussion about performance, architecture, interaction, and maintainability is encouraged.

### Expected Behavior

- Respect other people’s time and boundaries
- Use evidence when discussing performance and architecture
- Keep feedback specific and restrained
- Be honest about testing results and observations

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks
- Deliberate misinformation
- Unsupported performance claims
- Destructive changes

### Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, comments, or collaboration access when needed.
